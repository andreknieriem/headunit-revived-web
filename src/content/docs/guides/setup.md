Welcome to Headunit Revived! This guide will help you set up your Android device as an Android Auto headunit.

## Prerequisites

- A tablet or Android device running Android 4.1 or higher.
- A USB cable or a stable WiFi connection.
- Android Auto app installed on your phone.

## Installation

Currently, the app is in the testing phase. To install it:

1. **Join the community:** Join the [Google Group](https://groups.google.com/g/headunit-revived) to get access.
2. **Opt-in for testing:** Visit the [Play Store Testing Link](https://play.google.com/apps/testing/com.andrerinas.headunitrevived).
3. **Download:** Search for "Headunit Revived" in the Play Store on your tablet.

## First Start

When you open the app for the first time:
- Read and accept the **Safety Disclaimer**.
- Grant necessary permissions (USB access, etc.).
- You will see the main dashboard with buttons for different connection methods.

## Next Steps
